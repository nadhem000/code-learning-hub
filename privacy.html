Privacy Policy

Last Updated: February 10, 2024

We collect minimal data, store progress locally, never sell your information, and give you full control.

1 Information We Collect

We collect only the information necessary to provide and improve our educational services:

  • Learning data – lesson progress, saved code, preferences (stored locally in your browser).
  • Technical data – browser type, device information, pages visited (anonymous).

2 How We Use Your Information

Your information helps us personalize your learning, track progress, and improve the platform. We never sell your data.

3 Data Storage & Security

Most data stays in your browser's localStorage. You can export, clear, or back it up anytime. All connections are HTTPS.

4 Your Rights & Choices

You can reset progress, export data, or clear localStorage via browser settings. No account needed.

5 Third‑Party Services

We use only essential libraries (like Highlight.js) served via CDN. No analytics or tracking scripts.

6 Children's Privacy

Our content is suitable for all ages. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

7 Policy Changes

We may update this policy. The “last updated” date will always reflect the latest version.
